Dorapod — Owner's Manual

Overview
Dorapod is a hand-sized smart-assistant device shaped like a stylised
AirPods-style case.  It listens to the user's voice, scans the surrounding
environment with its onboard cameras and sensors, answers spoken
questions, and replies in audio.  Dorapod is a prototype unit — it is
NOT for sale.

Appearance
The body of Dorapod is a rounded, oval-shaped case made of light blue
soft-touch silicone, similar in form factor to a wireless-earbud charging
case.  The word "Doraemon" is embossed in slightly darker blue letters
across the upper face.  A horizontal red band runs across the front, just
above the middle.  Centred on the red band sits a small yellow bell with
a vertical slit down its face and a tiny loop on top — this bell is the
device's primary control button.  Below the red band, occupying most of
the lower front face, is a large white panel with a smile-shaped concave
curve cut into its lower edge — this is the front pocket.  On the right
side of the case, a small metallic carabiner-style clip and a short
silver chain dangle from the body; clipped to the end of the chain is the
"pancake pendant" — a circular, golden-brown puck about the size of a
coin with a darker brown disc inset at its centre, intended to evoke a
dorayaki pancake.  Dorapod's overall colour palette is light blue, red,
yellow, white, and pancake-brown.

Components
- Main body: light blue silicone case with embossed "Doraemon" text.
- Red band: horizontal accent stripe across the front of the case.
- Yellow bell: control button mounted on the red band.
- Front pocket: white smile-shaped recess on the lower front face,
  sized to hold the pancake pendant.
- Pancake pendant: round, two-tone brown disc shaped like a dorayaki,
  attached to the body by a short silver chain and a carabiner clip.
- Chain and clip: a small carabiner secures the pancake pendant to the
  right side of the case and lets the pendant be detached for charging.

How to use Dorapod
(1) Take the pancake pendant from the chain on the right side and slot
    it into the smile-shaped white front pocket on the front of the case.
    The pendant should sit flush with the surface of the pocket.
(2) Press the yellow bell on the red band once.  Dorapod will activate,
    begin scanning the environment with its onboard cameras and
    microphones, and listen for the user's voice.
(3) Speak the question or request out loud.  Dorapod will process the
    audio together with what it sees in the surroundings, then reply
    out loud through its built-in speaker.
(4) When finished, press the yellow bell again to stop Dorapod, or
    leave it idle — it will return to standby on its own.

Charging
Dorapod is powered by the pancake pendant.  To recharge:
(1) Detach the pancake pendant from the front pocket (or from its
    chain) and hold it firmly in the palm of your hand for about
    10 minutes.  The pendant warms up from body heat — this is what
    actually replenishes its charge.
(2) Once the pendant feels warm to the touch, slot it back into the
    front pocket of Dorapod.  The device is now ready to use again.
Note: charging by hand heat is the only supported method.  Do not place
the pendant on an external heater, stove, or hot surface.

Important notes
- Dorapod is a prototype unit and is NOT for sale.  Do not attempt to
  purchase or resell.
- The pancake pendant is required for operation.  Without the pendant
  inserted in the front pocket, pressing the yellow bell has no effect.
- Always keep the pendant attached to the carabiner chain when not in
  use so it is not lost.
